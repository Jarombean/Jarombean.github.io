Arcades!

Early arcade history

Arcades have been around for over 50 years!
The first arcade game was Computer Space released in 1971.
Arcades boomed throughout the late 70's and early 80's,
bringing us hits like Pac-man and Donkey Kong.
But in 1983, the North American videogame crash impacted the market.
Bringing an end to the golden age of arcades

A new Era

Arcades still existed of course. Arcade hardware got more and more powerful.
By the end of the Decade, 3D graphics were possible thanks to a new system from Namco
