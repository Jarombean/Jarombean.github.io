History

History is honestly super intersting to me, My favorite subject is the Rhodesian Bush war between 1965 and 1979.

the subject fascinates me, the idea of such a small country being shunned by the entire international community, warring against soviet-backed forces, and holding out for over a decade blows my mind!

What's the Rhodesian Bush war?

Rhodesia was a country in southern Africa. First founded in the 1890's by the British, rhodesia would gain self-governance in 1923. In the 1960's as the british were decololizing, a power vacuum formed. All across Africa, guerilla groups formed to fill the void. PM Ian Smith saw this was happening, and Unilateraly Declared Independence in 1965.
This resulted in international condemnation and soviet-backed guerillas marched into Rhodesia to stop the white-rule. Rhodesia held up for 15 years, finally turning over some power to moderate black groups in 1978, and to the soviet guerillas in 1980, Robert Mugabe ruled as a dictator until 2017, when a coup started by his own party overthrew him
